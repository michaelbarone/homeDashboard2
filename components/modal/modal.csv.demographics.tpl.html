CSV format explained for demographic file

All data is only stored within this browser session. No data will be sent or stored on any server for this application.
All Emplid fields are hashed when imported to help anonymise both student and instructor identities.

CSV example:

Emplid Gender Birth Year Ethnic Group Citizenship Application Year Admit Type Desc Father Educ Lvl Desc Mother Educ Lvl Desc ELM Status Desc EPT Status Desc
783089809 F 1976 Asian USA 2008-09 Transfer 2 - Some High School 3 - High School Graduate Exempt (GE Breadth) Exempt (Transfer)
743039216 M 1980 White USA 2007-08 First Time 7 - Postgraduate 5 - 2-Year College Graduate Exempt (SAT I) Exempt (SAT I)
555089809 M 1986 Black/African USA 2004-05 First Time 6 - 4 Year College Graduate 5 - 2-Year College Graduate Exempt (SAT I) Exempt (SAT I)






Emplid The student ID or any unique key to identify a student. This id should only occur 1 time in this data set.
Gender Gender of the Student: only use: [M, F, Unknown]
Birth Year Birth Year of the student
Ethnic Group Students' ethnic group as reported
Citizenship Country the student has main Citizenship in. [USA, Canada, Mexico, etc.]
Application Year The academic year the student applied to attend the college or transfered into the college.
Admit Type Desc The type of admission to the university: [First Time, Transfer, Ret Transfer, Continuing, Early Start, Transient, etc]
Father Educ Lvl Desc Highest Edu level of father: [1 - No High School,2 - Some High School,3 - High School Graduate,4 - Some College,5 - 2 Year College Graduate,6 - 4 Year College Graduate,7 - Postgraduate,8- No Response]
Mother Educ Lvl Desc Highest Edu level of mother: [1 - No High School,2 - Some High School,3 - High School Graduate,4 - Some College,5 - 2 Year College Graduate,6 - 4 Year College Graduate,7 - Postgraduate,8- No Response]
Total GPA Total GPA at start of this term
ELM Status Desc ELM status of student when entering university
EPT Status Desc EPT status of student when entering university
